Privacy Policy

How we handle the information you share with us through this website.

Last updated:

1. Introduction

Chats Bar & Cafe respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use and protect your personal information in line with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020. This is placeholder content provided as part of a website demo and should be reviewed before going live.

2. Information we collect

When you submit a booking enquiry or feedback form, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address and phone number
  • Booking details such as date, time and group size
  • Any message or feedback you choose to share

3. How we use your information

We use your information only to respond to your enquiry, manage your booking, and improve our service. We do not sell your information to anyone.

4. How your information is handled

Form submissions on this website are delivered to us by email via a third-party form service (FormSubmit). We keep your information only as long as needed to respond to your enquiry.

5. Cookies & analytics

This website may use basic cookies to support its functionality. You can control cookies through your browser settings at any time.

6. Your rights

You have the right to ask what personal information we hold about you, and to request that it be corrected or deleted. To do so, contact us using the details below.
