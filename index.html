About Chats

Your local for coffee, food and a quiet drink.

Chats Bar & Cafe is a laid-back spot in Christchurch built around the simple stuff done well — a good coffee, a plate of honest food and a welcome that makes you want to stay a bit longer.

Whether you're in for a quick flat white, a long catch-up over brunch or a cold one at the end of the day, you'll find the same friendly room and the same easy pace. Come as you are.

  • ✓ Locally owned and run, with a regular crowd of locals
  • ✓ Coffee, brunch and a full bar under one roof
  • ✓ Friendly, unhurried service from open to close
